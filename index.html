System Architecture

1. The AI-XR World Model That Sees and Operates

MedOS constructs a Agentic World Model that fundamentally understands surgical physics. We model the surgical procedure as a continuous State-Action-Transition loop:

S
INPUT

State (St)

Includes anatomical structures, tissue tension physics, field-of-view quality, and critical anatomical landmarks.

A
PROCESS

Action (At)

Decouples surgical maneuvers into universal primitives combined with physical parameters.

T
OUTPUT

Transition (T)

Predicts the evolution St → At → St+1. The core capability is simulating physical consequences (e.g., tearing from over-traction).

2. Dual-System Architecture

Mimicking the training path of human surgeons, we employ a "hindsight-driven" distillation paradigm.
