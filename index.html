Democratizing Expertise

One of the most impactful findings is the system's ability to level the playing field. In human-AI collaboration studies, MedOS enabled nurses and medical students to rival the diagnostic accuracy of attending physicians. Furthermore, it acted as a cognitive safety net, restoring the performance of post-call doctors to levels better than their well-rested baseline.
